ABOUT US

NASARA FOUNDATIONS can be categorized under the most prominent NGO groups in Ghana, and fas been thriving fir almost 5 years now. We were extablished on 4th March, 2020. We are located or based in the Northern part of Ghana and dedicated to grooming the youth to become citizens who have the Northern Region of Ghana and Ghana as a whole at heart.

WHAT WE DO

Over here at NASARA FOUNDATIONS we give out scholarships to needy but briLiant students. Our focus over the pas years has mostly been centered in the Norther Region because that is where it first originated. Since our establishment we have aided many northern students secure scholarships and we have many of them furthering their educations in countries all over the world.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to groom the youth to become citizens who have the Northern Region of Ghana and Ghana as a whole at heart.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to groom youths to have the mentality to create a better Ghana.
